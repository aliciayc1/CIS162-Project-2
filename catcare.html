When you adopt a cat, you must also keep in mind certain things to make sure your cat is properly cared for and loved. Otherwise, you may end up hurting your cat. Here are some basic things you should remember:

  • Don't yell or hit your cat to train it, it will just end up scaring them.
  • Make sure none of your plants are poisonous before putting them in your house.
  • In general, make sure to "cat-proof" your house before your cat first comes home.
  • Keep in mind your cat's boundaries and respect them- they do not always want to be pet.
  • However, make sure to be welcoming to your cat when it is first introduced to your house- it will be shy at first.
  • No matter how much media may encourage it, do not give your cat milk or yarn.
  • Never give your cat anything that is meant for dogs.
  • Make sure your house is stimulating enough for your cat.
