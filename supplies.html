Cat Food

It is ideal for your cat to have a diet consisting of both wet and dry food.

There are many things that you should buy before adopting a cat to ensure that they live comfortably and healthy. Examples are:
  • Canned and dry food
  • Cat litter
  • Litter box + scooper
  • Toys + scratching post
  • Bowls for food and water
  • Hair brush/comb
  • Collar
  • Bed/comfy resting area
  • Cat carrier
  • Cat toothbrush + toothpaste
You may also buy a climbing structure/cat tower for your cat, which can involve multiple things to keep them stimulated and relaxed. In addition, as cats clean themselves, you generally do not need to buy any bathing solution for them.
